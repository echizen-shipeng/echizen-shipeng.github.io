Content: Design and Production of a Beacon Tracking Intelligent Racing Car Based on MCU Simulator
Time: Oct. 2017 - Aug. 2018
Advisor: Prof. Zhiming Zhang

1.Introduction

  • We designed a smart vehicle using K60(MCU) and OV7620 camera in order to compete in beacon tracking group of National Intelligent Car Race. The task is that we should turn off ten beacons which will elluminate randomly as soon as possible with no collision with specific obstacle.

2.My contribution

  • Design and Production of a Beacon Tracking Intelligent Racing Car Based on MCU Oct. 2017 - Apr. 2018Designed the complete set of hardware from schematics to PCB layout using Altium
  • Identified the target beacon and obstacle by processing the image with adaptive threshold filtering algorithm
  • Optimized trajectory based on B-spline curve and designed a PID controller to follow the target trajectory
  • Won the First Prize in Beacon Tracking Group of the 2018 NXP Cup National College Student Smart Car Competition
