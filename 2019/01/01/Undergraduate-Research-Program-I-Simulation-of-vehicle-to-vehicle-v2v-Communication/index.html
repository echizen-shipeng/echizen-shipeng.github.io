Undergraduate Research Program I: Simulation of vehicle to vehicle (v2v) Communication

  • 2019-01-01
  • ShipengLiu
Content: Simulation of vehicle to vehicle (v2v) Communication
Time: Oct. 2018 - Jan. 2019
Advisor: Prof. Ping Wang

1.Introduction

  • Based on 3GPP LTE physical layer related protocols and v2v (vehicle to vehicle communication) related channel model, a matlab-based LTEv2v link-level simulation platform was established. The essence of v2v communication realized in this platform is LTE d2d communication under high-speed movement, which reuses the frequency band resources of LTE uplink. I also designed the user UI interface for the platform, and proposed a new snr-cqi (MCS) mapping model based on the higher block error rate (BLER) and delay requirements of v2v on the basis of the simulation platform, to better realize the link adaptation in v2v mode. The improved simulation results are compared with the fixed CQI (MCS) scheme and the original uplink mapping scheme. Finally, the effectiveness of the scheme is verified and the link performance under multi-vehicle condition is verified.

2.My contribution

  • Created a v2v channel model based on the LTE-A Downlink Link Level Simulator and designed a GUI interface for it
  • Designed a SNR-CQI mapping algorithm of signal modulation and stroke a better balance of bit error ratio and bit rate
